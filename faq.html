Pest Control

Residential clients: We currently cover the entire Gauteng Province on a full-time basis, and Polokwane on an ad-hoc basis.

Commercial clients: We offer our services across Gauteng and Limpopo, as well as surrounding areas.

Not sure if we cover your area? Call or WhatsApp us on 060 200 4890 and we'll confirm availability immediately.

We treat all common pests including:

  • Crawling insects — cockroaches, ants, spiders, silverfish
  • Flying insects — flies, mosquitoes, wasps
  • Bedbugs — full eradication with follow-up
  • Rodents — rats and mice (extermination and exclusion)
  • Termites — structural treatment and protection
  • Stored product pests — grain beetles, weevils

Not sure if we cover your problem? Call or WhatsApp us and we'll advise immediately.

Our proven 5-step procedure ensures thorough, lasting results:

  • Pre-inspection — identifying where the infestation is located and its severity
  • Flushing — flushing out pests using a flushing agent spray to expose hidden insects
  • Fumigation — treating the property using a pressure sprayer and fogging machine
  • Gel bait application — applying gel bait around appliances, counters, and hinges
  • Treatment report — issuing a full treatment report with recommendations

For standard residential properties the fumigation takes 30 to 45 minutes. Larger commercial properties or severe infestations may require a full day or multiple visits. We provide a clear time estimate before every appointment and will not leave until the job is done properly.

We offer 24/7 emergency support for urgent pest situations. Contact us immediately via WhatsApp or phone on 060 200 4890 and we will prioritise your case and respond as quickly as possible — often the same day.

Yes — you need to step outside during the service. You can return immediately afterwards. Simply open all windows and doors to allow air to circulate and ventilate the space.

You can re-enter immediately after the service is complete. Open all windows to allow for ventilation — the smell is minimal and will be completely gone within 30 minutes.

It typically takes about 20 days for the treatment to reach full effect due to the pesticide's 20-day residual action. The increased pest activity you may notice in the first few days is actually a positive sign — hidden cockroaches are being flushed out as they contact the chemical and emerge before dying.

A decrease in numbers will start soon after treatment and continue steadily over the next 10–15 days.

To ensure a safe and effective treatment, please complete these steps before our team arrives:

  • Cover or store food items — seal or store all open foodstuffs to prevent contamination
  • Remove pets and children — keep pets and children out of the treated area during service
  • Protect tropical fish — cover fish tanks with a damp cloth and turn off air pumps
  • Open cupboards and drawers — leave cupboard drawers and doors open for full access
  • Clear clutter — remove items from floors and surfaces to allow thorough treatment
  • Notify us of concerns — inform us of any allergies, sensitivities, or specific areas needing attention

To ensure the best results and keep your space safe and comfortable, please follow these steps after treatment:

  • Ventilate — open windows and doors to let fresh air circulate before returning indoors
  • Wash utensils — wash all cooking and eating utensils thoroughly before using them again
  • Leave treated surfaces — allow the treatment to dry completely before wiping down surfaces for maximum effectiveness
  • Wait before replacing items — wait until the treatment has dried before putting items back in place
  • Leave gel baits undisturbed — avoid moving or wiping gel baits placed behind or inside appliances, as they are actively eliminating pests
